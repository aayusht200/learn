Projects

Straw|Hat Pirates

The Straw Hat Pirates are a quirky and tight-knit crew of ten (currently) led by Monkey D. Luffy, a rubber-bodied pirate with the dream of becoming the King of the Pirates. They sail the Grand Line aboard their ship, the Thousand Sunny, each member pursuing their own unique dreams while fiercely loyal to their captain and each other. They are known for their strong bonds, outlandish adventures, and growing notoriety in the world.

Team Members

Monkey D Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, "Straw Hat" Luffy, is the rubber-bodied captain of the Straw Hat Pirates in One Piece. He dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates by finding the legendary treasure, One Piece, and is fiercely loyal to his crew.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, "Pirate Hunter," is the fiercely loyal and incredibly skilled three-sword-wielding swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates, aiming to become the world's strongest swordsman. He's known for his serious demeanor, terrible sense of direction, and unwavering dedication to his captain and crew.

Blackleg Sanji

"Black Leg" Sanji is the Straw Hat Pirates' suave and chivalrous cook, known for his incredibly powerful kick-based "Black Leg Style" and his fiery "Diable Jambe" technique. He dreams of finding the All Blue and fiercely protects his crew and any woman in need.
